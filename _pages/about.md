I am a first-year Ph.D. student at Tsinghua Embodied AI Lab (TEA Lab) affiliated to [Institute for Interdisciplinary Information Sciences (IIIS), Tsinghua University](https://iiis.tsinghua.edu.cn) under the supervision of Prof. [Huazhe Xu](http://hxu.rocks/).

Before that, I received my Bachelor's Degree at [AI class, Shanghai Jiao Tong University](https://aisjtu.icu) where I was advised by Prof. [Cewu Lu](https://www.mvig.org). In the summer of 2023, I shorted visited Prof. [Hao Su](https://cseweb.ucsd.edu/~haosu/) at UC San Diego.

My research interest lies in the interdisciplinary topics of robotics, 3D vision, and reinforcement learning.
